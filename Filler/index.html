Category

Transparent Fillers
Transparent fillers are additives used in polymers to enhance properties like mechanical strength and thermal stability while preserving optical clarity. They are crucial in industries requiring both aesthetic appeal and enhanced performance, such as automotive, electronics, packaging, and medical devices.
TPT Fillers
TPT Fillers, sourced from natural minerals, enhance polymer products by improving tensile strength, imparting a translucent appearance, providing anti-blocking properties, and enhancing processability. Widely utilized in automotive, packaging, construction, and consumer goods industries, they effectively reduce shrinkage and offer cost-effective solutions with superior surface smoothness and thermal stability.
Bio Fillers
Bio Fillers represent a cutting-edge solution in sustainable materials, crafted from biodegradable components like modified CaCO3 powder and bioplastics. These fillers enhance product properties such as gloss and film hardness while reducing production costs. Ideal for biodegradable packaging, injection molding, and more, they offer eco-friendliness, cost-effectiveness, and regulatory compliance, making them a versatile choice across industries.
Calcium Fillers
Calcium Carbonate Fillers blend ultrafine CaCO3 with PE/PP/HD polymers, offering grades with 15% to 30% polymer content. Our masterbatches, available in blue, yellow, and green, cater to diverse applications like film, molding, and thermoforming. Benefits include superior dispersion, consistent quality, optimal loading for strength, and specialized formulations for specific uses in PS, HIPS, PP, and more, enhancing product durability, printability, and cost-effectiveness.
