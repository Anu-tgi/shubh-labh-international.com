  • 1. Combination of specialized pigments

Additives Masterbatches
At SLI, our Additive Masterbatch solutions enhance plastics with specialized properties. Masterbatches are concentrated mixtures of additives that improve plastics' performance. Our range includes Anti-Static, Anti-Corrosive, Flame Retardant, UV Stabilizer, and Desiccant additives. Using our masterbatches ensures improved productivity, reduced material costs, and enhanced product quality. Trust SLI's expertise and high-quality materials for customized additive masterbatch solutions that meet your specific needs, enhancing your manufacturing process and product performance.
