Bio Filler

Introduction

As one of the premier offerings within our expansive product range, SLI proudly presents our bio fillers, standing as one of the four front-runners in our lineup. Bio fillers represent a cutting-edge solution in the realm of sustainable materials, embodying our commitment to environmental responsibility and innovation. Crafted from biodegradable materials, these fillers offer a forward-thinking approach to product development, seamlessly blending eco-friendliness with performance. With SLI's bio fillers, customers can achieve both their sustainability goals and high-quality standards, making them an indispensable choice for environmentally-conscious applications.

Features

  • Eco-Friendly: Bio fillers are made from biodegradable materials, making them environmentally sustainable and reducing the carbon footprint of end-products.
  • Biodegradability: These fillers degrade naturally over time, minimizing environmental impact and reducing waste accumulation.
  • Cost-Effective: Bio fillers offer a cost-effective solution compared to traditional fillers, providing value without compromising performance.
  • Versatility: They can be used across various industries and applications, offering flexibility in product design and development.
  • Compliance: Bio fillers meet regulatory standards for biodegradability and environmental sustainability, ensuring compliance with industry regulations and standards.

Applications

  • Blow Moulding, Toys, CDs, Safety Products, Cosmetic Packaging materials, Fashion Products, Packaging and Films, Automotive and Electrical Applications, Spectatcle Frames
  • Swatches, Telephone Controls, Doorbell and all kinds of Dark Signs, Traffic, Sail, Toys
  • Siding & Cladding, Composite Decking, Fencing, Decking and Railing, Windows & Doors, Flooring, Roofing, Window Blinds
  • HDPE optical fibre ducts, Household products, Furniture industry
