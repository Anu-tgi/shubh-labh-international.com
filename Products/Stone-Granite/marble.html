Stone/Granite/Marble Masterbatch

Introduction

Discover the exceptional quality of our Stone/Granite/Marble Masterbatch, designed to deliver stunning visual effects for your plastic products. Ideal for injection molding, extrusion, and blow molding, our masterbatch ensures high dispersion, color consistency, and UV stability, making it the perfect choice for creating elegant, durable finishes that mimic natural stone, granite, and marble.

Available in PE, PET, PC, PMMA, ABS, TPU, PBT, Nylon, Bio Compostable and PVC Carrier Base Polymer.

Features

  • Granite or marble is used to an important smooth and mirror-like finish to the product, along with this it also improves abrasion to the product.
  • Along with sparkling finish, it also provides lubricity, reduces coefficient of friction to the final product.
  • Tailored Formulations: We offer customizable formulations to meet specific requirements
  • Improves abrasion resistance to the finish product.
  • With our granite effect, we have developed an impressive-looking masterbatch solution for colouring polymers. This creates the attractive swirling patterns associated with marble.
  • Capable of developing customized colors tailored to meet specific customer needs and preferences.

Applications

  • Office and Stationary Goods, Household Products
  • Interior Design, Cosmetic Containers, Soap Dispensers, Electronics
  • Bottles, Blow Moulding, Film Application, Automotive Parts
  • Furniture, Tableware, Kitchenware, Injection Moulding
