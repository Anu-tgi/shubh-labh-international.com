PMMA Masterbatch

Introduction

We produce PC and PMMA masterbatches based on PC and PMMA itself instead of using another base, like most available products in the market. The method of our manufacturing and colouring allows 100% light to pass giving utmost transparency. PE Masterbatches are harmful in colouring ABS with the destruction of shine as well.

Features

  • Superior Mechanical Properties: Our PMMA masterbatches offer enhanced performance, making them suitable for demanding applications that require strength and rigidity.
  • Chemical Resistance: Our PMMA masterbatches exhibit excellent resistance to chemicals, oils, and abrasion, ensuring the integrity of the end products even in harsh environments.
  • Tailored Formulations: We offer customizable formulations to meet specific requirements
  • Consistent Quality: Our PMMA masterbatches undergo stringent quality control measures to ensure consistency and uniformity
  • Technical Support: We provide comprehensive technical support and expertise to assist our customers in optimizing their processes and achieving the best results

Applications

  • Blow Moulding, Point-of-Purchase (POP) Displays, CDs, Safety Products, Cosmetic Optical Components, Fashion Products, Packaging and Films, Automotive and Electrical Applications, Spectatcle Frames
  • Swatches, Telephone Controls, Doorbell and all kinds of Dark Signs, Traffic, Sail, Point-of-Purchase (POP) Displays
  • Siding & Cladding, Composite Decking, Fencing, Decking and Railing, Windows & Doors, Flooring, Roofing, Window Blinds
  • HDPE optical fibre ducts, Household products, Furniture industry
