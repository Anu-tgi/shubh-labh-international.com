Wood Grain Masterbatch

Introduction

Selected pigments and special molding techniques are combined to produce characteristics akin to broad patterns seen in natural wood grains. The wood-grain-like wood-plastic composite material boasts a simulative wood appearance, featuring natural and distinct grain patterns, vibrant surface color, and an impressive wood-grain-like effect. As a result, it represents an ideal wood-like wood-plastic product, offering the aesthetic appeal of wood with the added benefits of composite materials.

Features

  • Low Toner Consumption: It requires minimal toner consumption, leading to cost savings and efficient use of resources.
  • Favorable Temperature Tolerance: The masterbatch exhibits good temperature tolerance
  • Excellent Dispersibility: It disperses evenly within the base material, ensuring uniform coloration and enhancing the quality of the final product.
  • Ease of Operation and Control: the masterbatch is easy to operate and control, simplifying the manufacturing process
  • Favorable Compatibility: The masterbatch demonstrates excellent compatibility with PVC-based base materials

Applications

  • Blow Moulding, Toys, CDs, Safety Products, Cosmetic Bottles, Fashion Products, Packaging and Films, Automotive and Electrical Applications, Spectatcle Frames
  • Swatches, Telephone Controls, Doorbell and all kinds of Dark Signs, Traffic, Sail, Toys
  • Siding & Cladding, Composite Decking, Fencing, Decking and Railing, Windows & Doors, Flooring, Roofing, Window Blinds
  • HDPE optical fibre ducts, Household products, Furniture industry
