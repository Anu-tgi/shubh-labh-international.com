PET Masterbatch

Introduction

SLI Company is a reputable manufacturer, exporter of PET masterbatches. Poly Ethylene terephthalate - commonly known as PET - is the one of the most widely used polymers. These masterbatches are especially designed for the PET bottle industry. Fibre, sheets, BOPET films and monofilament. The applications should be a sub-heading of the product heading.

Features

  • Color Consistency: PET masterbatches ensure consistent coloration throughout the polymer, maintaining uniformity in the end products.
  • Heat Stability: These masterbatches offer heat stability, allowing PET materials to withstand high temperatures during processing without degradation.
  • UV Protection: PET masterbatches provide UV protection, preserving the integrity and appearance of PET products exposed to sunlight.
  • Improved Mechanical Properties: PET masterbatches enhance the mechanical properties of PET materials, including strength, toughness, and dimensional stability.
  • Processability: PET masterbatches are formulated for ease of incorporation and processing, ensuring smooth production processes and high-quality end products.

Applications

  • Blow Moulding, thin films & coatings, CDs, Safety Products, Cosmetic Bottles and Containers, Fashion Products, Packaging and Films, Automotive and Electrical Applications, Spectatcle Frames
  • Swatches, Telephone Controls, Doorbell and all kinds of Dark Signs, Traffic, Sail, thin films & coatings
  • Siding & Cladding, Composite Decking, Fencing, Decking and Railing, Windows & Doors, Flooring, Roofing, Window Blinds
  • HDPE optical fibre ducts, Household products, Furniture industry
