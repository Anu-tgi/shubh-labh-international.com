MY MESSAGE

It is with great pride and a sense of achievement that I reflect on the journey of Singhal Plastic since its inception in 1979. Our firm, registered in 1986, has grown from a small venture into a leading force in the Indian polymer industry. This journey has been marked by relentless innovation, technological advancements, and a steadfast commitment to quality and sustainability.

I am a post-graduate from Delhi University and have always been driven by a passion for innovation. Throughout my academic career, I was a scholar, and this zeal for learning and creating has fueled my professional endeavours. My initial foray into the polymer industry began with advancements in the Polystyrene sector. Our innovations in this area set new standards and paved the way for our future successes.

Our focus then shifted to the Polypropylene industry, where we continued to break new ground. Each step of the way, our mission was clear: to revolutionize the polymer industry with cutting-edge products and solutions. Our journey has not been just about creating new products but also about reimagining how we approach polymer production and usage. One of our most significant achievements has been in the realm of color. Today, Singhal Plastic boasts an impressive portfolio of over 4,000 colors, setting us apart as a leader in the polymer industry. Our commitment to innovation has been constant, with a creative mind that never rests.

Aware of the environmental hazards posed by single-use plastics, we adopted a use-reuse policy early on. This policy focuses on managing polymer waste effectively, ensuring it can be reused to create new polymer-based products. Our efforts have not only been about reducing waste but also about creating sustainable, eco-friendly solutions.

In recent years, our journey to save the environment has led us to develop breakthrough biodegradable materials. These innovations make polymers more sustainable and biodegradable, aligning with our mission to protect and preserve nature. Environmental sustainability has always been a top priority for us, and we remain committed to finding new ways to manage waste and promote a greener, cleaner future.

As we continue to grow and evolve, I am reminded of two of my favorite quotes: "always take the bull by its horns" and "keep breaking new ground." These principles guide our work and inspire us to push the boundaries of what is possible.

I am also proud to be an active member of the All India Plastic Association, where we collaborate with industry peers to drive forward the future of polymers in India. Thank you for your continued support and partnership. Together, we can achieve great things and make a significant impact on our industry and the environment.
