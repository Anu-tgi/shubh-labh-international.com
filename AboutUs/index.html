About the Company

With a legacy spanning close to four decades, Shubh Labh International stands as a paramount figure in the global polymer industry. Since our inception in 1986, we've harnessed our profound expertise to pioneer innovative and top-tier solutions for clients across the globe. At Shubh Labh International, we're your one-stop destination for all polymer solutions - masterbatch, thermoplastics, biocompostables, compounding, fillers and much more. Since 2016, our focus has been on creating tailored solutions to meet our clients' diverse needs.

Our product range includes Additive and Special Effect Masterbatches, Calcium Filler, and Bio Fillers, offering a wide selection of over 4000 color shades, including all RAL and PANTONE codes. Shubh Labh International is here to meet all your masterbatch requirements. Our commitment to quality and precision testing sets us apart in the industry.
